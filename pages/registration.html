Registration Information

(1) Author Registration

① Registration
  • Author Registration fees are not refundable.
  • The author registration deadline is 3 March 2023.
  • Each registration package includes access to the paper, keynote and workshops, poster reception, coffee breaks, lunches (on Day2 to Day 3), with one banquet ticket.
  • Additional banquet tickets (KRW 100,000 each) can be purchased up to one.
  • For an invitation letter or a visa support letter for your visa application, please find them in the visa information page.
  • You can choose vegetarian dietary when you are registering for a conference.
② Paper Information/Rules
  • Each paper (Full Paper and VisNotes) must have at least one full registration whether the registrant is a student or not.
  • Each full registration is valid for one paper (Full Paper, and VisNotes).
  • One author for each paper to be published in IEEE Xplore (i.e., full paper, VisNotes paper) MUST register under the Author Registration category.
  • Each Author Registration is valid for ONE paper.
  • The author registering for the paper does not need to be the same as the paper presenter.
  • Posters and associated event papers not published in IEEE Xplore do not need to have an Author Registration.

(2) Full Registration

  • Each registration package includes access to the paper, keynote, poster reception, coffee breaks, lunches (on Day2 to Day 3), with one banquet ticket.
  • Additional banquet tickets (KRW 100,000 each) can be purchased up to one.
  • For an invitation letter or a visa support letter for your visa application, please find them in the visa information page.
  • You can choose vegetarian dietary when you are registering for a conference.

(3) Cancellation Policy

  • All refund/cancellation requests must be received in writing to pvis_reg@pvis.org by 20 March, 2023, 11:59 PM AoE TIME. There will be an administrative fee of KRW 130,000 for cancelled registrations.
  • You may only cancel your registration before the cancellation deadline.
  • Author registration is not refundable.
